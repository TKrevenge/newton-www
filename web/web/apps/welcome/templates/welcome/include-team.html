Jizhe Xu is the Founder and Chairman of the Newton Foundation, and the Co-founder of Elastos. In 2005, he founded the open source software community Zeuux. In 2011, he joined the e-commerce company Okbuy.com worked as CTO. In 2014, he began his own entrepreneurial projects such as 3D printing, intelligent hardware, mobile game, talent recruitment and so on. As a pioneer and promoter of the free open source software, Jizhe Xu is committed to developing free software and promoting the application of free software in enterprises, especially in Internet applications and network security fields. Meanwhile, he actively advocates enterprises feedback to the free software community.
